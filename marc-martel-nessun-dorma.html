Marc Martel - Nessun Dorma

Another great song from the singer Marc Martel. If you haven't heard anything from him yet, then get onto Youtube and listen to some of his stuff.

I am becoming a big fan of this guy. His range of singing is brilliant and I hope that he starts to put up a lot more new material since his Queen Extravaganza hit.
